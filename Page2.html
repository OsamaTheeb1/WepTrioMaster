Frequently Asked Questions to Learn More About Our Services

What types of cars are available for rent?

We offer a variety of cars including sports cars, family cars, and economical cars.

What is the daily rental cost?

The daily rental cost starts from 50 SAR depending on the car type.

Are there discounts for long-term rentals?

Yes, we offer discounts on long-term rentals up to 20% based on the rental duration.

What are the conditions for picking up the car?

You must have a valid driver’s license and either a passport or ID card.

Can new drivers rent cars?

Yes, new drivers can rent cars provided they have had a valid driver’s license for at least 6 months.

What are the working hours of the exhibition?

Our working hours are from 9 AM to 6 PM every day of the week.

Do you offer car delivery service to homes?

Yes, we offer car delivery service to homes for an additional fee.

What documents are required to rent a car?

The required documents include a driver’s license, ID card or passport, and a credit card.

Does the insurance cover accidents?

Yes, the insurance covers accidents, and you can get additional coverage for more risks.

How can I book a car in advance online?

You can book through our website or by contacting our customer service center.
